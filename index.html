❓ Help & FAQ

How is my data saved?

Everything is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to a server, so your data stays private to this device. Clearing your browser data will reset the app.

How does income work?

Use the Income card on the Dashboard. Switch between "Hourly Wage" mode (wage × hours/week) or "Monthly Income" mode if you're paid a flat salary — we'll calculate the rest automatically.

What's a recurring expense?

Check the "Recurring" box when adding an expense (like rent or subscriptions) and it will automatically appear in every new month going forward, so you don't have to re-enter it.

How do category budget limits work?

Set a limit per category in Budget Planner. When your spending in that category gets close to (85%+) or exceeds the limit, you'll see a warning badge on the Dashboard and Reports pages.

Does the currency converter need internet?

Yes — it fetches live exchange rates from a public API. If you're offline, it will use the last successfully fetched rate for that currency pair if available.

How do I switch months or years?

Use the Year and Month dropdowns in the top bar. Recurring expenses automatically carry forward when you move to a new month.

What does "Log Out" do?

Since MoneyWise doesn't use accounts, Log Out simply clears your locally saved data and resets the app to a fresh start. Use it if you want to start over or hand the device to someone else.
